Frequently Asked
Questions

What is Wim?

Wim is the vend-and-return bottled beverage system that offers all your favorite refreshments, 100% waste-free.

What is Wim Unlimited?

Wim Unlimited is a monthly subscription that gives you unlimited access to all the refreshments you love. For $19/month, you’re free to enjoy as many Wim beverages as you want. All we ask is that you make sure to return all the empty bottles.

What type of drinks do you carry?

Wim currently offers a selection of still and sparkling waters in a variety of flavors. We’re always adding new beverages, so please let us know if there’s anything specific you’d like to see/sip.

Why do I have to return the empty bottle?

Instead of relying on the energy-intensive process of recycling, Wim glass bottles are designed to be returned to the Wim machine, where they are washed, sanitized, and kept in production. This is how we keep both our environmental impact and prices as low as possible.
